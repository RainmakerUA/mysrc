Since 30.09.2013 introduced Bonus program for passangers of 1st class wagons trains «INTERCITY+»
Issuance of travel documents, previously booked by Internet, can be performed in the ticket offices of JSC “Ukrzaliznytsia” on the territory where governmental authorities exercise their power on a full scale
In case of traveling along the territory of Ukraine in the coach 29M Kyiv – Prague, Kyiv – Bratislava of the train 771 Kyiv – Khmelnytsky
service «Bed linen» can be ordered only in the ticket offices
Travel documents for traveling along the territory of Ukraine by the trains from the CIS countries can be purchased only in the ticket offices
